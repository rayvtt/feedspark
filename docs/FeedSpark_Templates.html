ID Methodology

The "how we work" explainer — Identify → Deliver. Use in proposals, kick-offs and when a stakeholder asks what FeedSpark actually does.
Subject

How we work — the FeedSpark methodology

Body

Hi [Contact],

A short note on how we approach [Client]'s feed — our Identify → Deliver method:

1. Identify — audit the feed against a Golden Record (99.9% attribute-completeness target) and map intent: what your customers actually search for.
2. Structure — titles in MASK format (Brand + Material + Fit + Colour + Use-case), structured data, and conversational attributes (Q&A, variant option, related product).
3. Test — controlled A/B on titles, overlays and keywords; every hypothesis returns a result, win or lose.
4. Scale — winners roll out across markets, and AI-Ready feeds prepare you for ChatGPT Shopping, Perplexity and Google AI Mode.

We measure by uplift, not activity — the numbers report themselves back to you each review.

Best,
[Your name]

Tip: this doubles as the opening slide narrative for a new-business deck — same four steps, same order.
