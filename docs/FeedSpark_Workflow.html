Brief pipeline

  
Every brief from send to sign-off. Cards are compact so 20+ briefs stay visible: brand chip (+market if not GB), due, and days-in-court (red = over SLA — the lane tells you whose court). Hover for the full task; drag between lanes; click to open the ticket. The court cards show which of the three teams holds the ball — click one to spotlight its tickets. Paste any reply into the router and it finds the right ticket (by [ibfcode], brief ID or task wording), logs it, and moves the stage.
